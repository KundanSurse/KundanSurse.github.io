Kundan Surse

Doctoral Researcher

A curious physics student...

I am a doctoral researcher working on Semiconductor Quantum Computing. At Proff. Rahman's Group at University of New South Wales(UNSW), Sydney I am trying to improve coherence of Donor spin qubit for scalability of Quantum Computers. Our Group mainly works on computatinal modeling of semiconductor lattice, using Tight-Binding method and points out decoherence mechanisms for spin Qubits.

Prior to joining Prof. Rahman's Group I was working as a Scientist-SC at Sapce Application Center, Indian Space Research Organization(ISRO). In this role I had a responsibility of Designing, Testing and Delivery of Radio Frequency subsystem for Communication and Navigation Satellites.
